What is {{ title }} Paper Search Tool?

This tool can search {{ title }} papers on AAAI, ACL Conferences (ACL, IJCNLP, and NAACL), CVF Conferences (CVPR, ICCV, and WACV), ECVA (ECCV), ICLR, ICML, and NeurIPS, from 2017 and on.
Its main features are:

  • Each paper has embedded vectors generated by Facebook Fasttext, so that you can explore similar papers.
  • Our search uses papers' titles and abstracts. You can also specify the conference and year to search for with # and filter results that do not contain a word with - (e.g.: deep learning -attention #neurips #2019).
  • For multiple keywords, our search results will contain all the keywords and be sorted by word frequency. It will also consider in the search related words.
